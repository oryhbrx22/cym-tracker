When developing for this project:
- Always prioritize mobile responsiveness using Tailwind's `sm:`, `md:`, `lg:` prefixes.
- Ensure buttons and touch targets are large enough (min 40x40px for critical actions).
- Use `overflow-x-auto` for horizontally scrolling containers (like charts or wide lists) on small screens.
- Avoid fixed widths that might break on 320px-375px screens.
